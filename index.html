How it works

From blank layer to
polished copy in four steps.

A workflow that fits how you already design. No tab-switching, no copy-paste back-and-forth.

01
Select a text layer
Click any text element. UX Copywriter auto-loads existing content as context.
02
Describe the scenario
Type what the copy needs to say — e.g. "Friendly error for failed payment."
03
Pick a tone & generate
Choose your tone. Claude returns 5 variations in roughly one second.
04
Apply in one click
Hit Apply. The text layer updates instantly — no copy, no paste, no switching.
Tone library

Every message,
the right voice.

Three distinct tones — engineered for different emotional contexts. Switch in a click and regenerate instantly.

◼
Short & Simple
Functional and direct. Zero filler. Designed for confirmations, status messages, and system feedback.
◎
Empathetic
Warm and human. Avoids blame. Ideal for error states, failed transactions, and moments that need reassurance.
▲
Encouraging
Forward-looking and motivating. Perfect for onboarding, progress milestones, and empty states that invite action.

Features

Built for how
designers actually work.

One-click apply
Click any result card to push it directly to your selected text layer. No clipboard juggling — layer updates in real time.
~1 second generation
Powered by Groq's ultra-fast inference. Results appear in roughly a second — fast enough to not break your flow state.
Zero tracking
No analytics, no data collection, no logging. Your scenarios stay between you and the API. Keys stored locally in Figma.
Custom rules
Add product constraints like "max 40 chars" or "never say 'easy'" — every generated option respects them, every time.
Context-aware
Select a text layer and the plugin pre-fills the input with your existing copy — rewrites stay grounded in actual design intent.
Dark mode native
Full light/dark toggle baked in. Comfortable in bright daylight or 2am design sessions.
